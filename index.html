Take The
ROF Journey

Welcome to the ROF journey! – a practical, step by step guide towards a more sustainable lifestyle. Journey with us through 4 distinct levels of sustainable solutions with a focus on maximum impact for minimum effort. The responsibility of the planet is in our hands. We’ve created the map, so strap on your hiking shoes and get ready to explore the ROF journey to a more sustainable lifestyle

Level 1

The Explorer

The explorer is concerned about environmental issues and is interested in how they can reduce their footprint. Start the journey by learning the basics of a zero waste lifestyle.

Learn about zero waste

Learning the basics of a zero waste lifestyle is a great place to start our journey towards sustainability

Refuse and reduce single-used item

Let’s start with the basic tenants of zero waste - refusing the 5 most problematic items for our environment

Level 2

The Zero-waster

The zero-waster has learned the basics and is ready to incorporate the zero waste lifestyle into their homes and beyond.

learn to compost
and reduce foodwaste

Discarded food scraps make up to 60% of the average household bin. Learning to compost can significantly reduce the impact this has on landhill

ZW food shopping

Learn how to reduce packaging in the kitchen by buying bulk food style

DIY Products

Here is a crucial step in the ROF journey - learn to make your own household products.

parenting

The zero-waste journey doesn't end with us, learn practical solutions for going zero waste with pets and children.

Level 3

The Conscious Consumer

The conscious consumer has already made a significant impact by learning about the zero waste lifestyle. But this is only one piece of the puzzle in reducing our footprint. Where to now?

Incorporate a sustainable diet

With an endless array of food options available to us, which dietary choices are the most sustainable options?

become energy smart

Learn intelligent energy management with the type and amount of energy we use on a daily basis.

Embrace sustainable fashion

Learn the impact of sustainable fashion and how we can avoid advocating for this harmful industry.

travel smart

Whether you travel frequently or only once a year, learn some travel smart tips to help reduce your impact of traveling.

Practice minimalism

This is the ultimate step in the ROF journey. By following the above stages, the ROF journey has created an excellent foundation for now incorporating the practice of minimalism.

Level 4

The Activist

The activist is a passionate individual, who has completed the ROF journey and now wishes to help others to do the same.

contributing to rof

Our sincerest congratulations. You've put in a lot of effort to get this far. If you've followed all the steps in the ROF journey you have now significantly reduced your footprint. But the journey doesn't end here - help us to help others to do the same.
