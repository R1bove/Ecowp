Take The
ROF Journey

Welcome to the ROF journey! – a practical, step by step guide towards a more sustainable lifestyle. One of the hardest things with sustainability is knowing where to start. The ROF journey aims to solve this problem by taking you on a journey through 4 distinct levels of practical solutions. We believe in the philosophy of maximum impact for minimum effort, so you can start reducing your footprint immediately with solutions in level 1, then move through the higher levels at your own pace.

Our emphasis is on individual action. We can’t wait for politicians, corporations, green technologies, or even not-for-profits like us to solve the environmental crisis. The solution is within our own actions.We’ve created the map, so strap on your hiking shoes and get ready to explore the ROF journey to a more sustainable lifestyle.

Let’s start by learning about the zero-waste lifestyle.

Level 3

The Conscious Consumer

The conscious consumer has already made a significant impact by learning about the zero waste lifestyle. But this is only one piece of the puzzle in reducing our footprint. Where to now?

Incorporate a sustainable diet

The Activist
